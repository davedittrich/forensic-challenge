[Q1]
Nov 7 23:11:06  Portscans, portmapper requests 
      23:11:50 Shellcode attack with several %n in the attack string, 
               looks like format bug  attack against statd.

A root shell on Port 4545 is added to /etc/inetd.conf; inetd is being
restarted.  A quick search on the Internet reveals that this type of
attack closely matches the statd attack described in
http://www.cert.org/advisories/CA-2000-17.html
From a technical viewpoint, format bug exploits are very interesting,
but due to time limits I can't describe them in detail here.
